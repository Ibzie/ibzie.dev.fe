2025 Preprint

Multi-Perspective SDF-VAE

A variational autoencoder that learns compact 3D shape representations from multiple 2D perspectives using signed distance functions. We introduce a novel multi-view encoding scheme that reconstructs high-fidelity geometry from sparse viewpoints, outperforming single-view baselines on ShapeNet and custom multi-perspective benchmarks.

2025 Preprint

MoE Circuit Tracing: ALF vs AL

A comparative study of attribution-based (ALF) and activation-based (AL) methods for circuit discovery in Mixture-of-Experts transformers. We benchmark both approaches on fidelity, completeness, and computational efficiency, revealing trade-offs that inform best practices for mechanistic interpretability in sparse architectures.
